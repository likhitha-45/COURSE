About My College

Vishnu Institute for Technology, Bhimavaram was established under the aegis of Sri Vishnu Educational Society in the year 2008. The institute is ranked 180th for B.Tech by IndiaToday 2020 and ranked 123rd for B.Tech by The Week 2020. VIT Bhimavaram offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in the discipline of Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering and many more. Admission is based on marks obtained in the qualifying exam. The 2020 batch has witnessed a growth in the placement statistics from 88% (2018) to 97% (2020). The highest salary package offered to students of the 2021 outgoing batch is INR 41 LPA and the lowest salary package is offered at INR 3 LPA. Some of the potential recruiters are Hexaware, Accenture, Amazon, IBM, Hyundai, Infosys, Capgemini, etc.

Carrom is a popular indoor game, believed to have originated in India in the 18th century. Widely played across the Indian subcontinent, Carrom requires a good sense of angles and superlative precision and concentration. It was once played − mostly by children and ladies − for amusement, but now it is being played at competitive level in an organized manner.
